ROOMS

'Rooms' was my first project at NCN. Our project aim was to create a 2D game in GameMaker Studio. It was a 3rd person, horror-esque, puzzle and mystery game where the aim was to escape a mansion whilst also finding out what had happened. As this was my first project using this software, I had many hardships to overcome. Many involved the time frame: only six weeks of production. After realising that I wasn't going to be able to create what I planned, I had to cut out most of my idea.
